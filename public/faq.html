Frequently Asked Questions

What is FundMyFuture?▶

FundMyFuture is a platform that helps communities fund micro-scholarships for local students.

How do I apply for funding as a student?▶

Students can create a profile, submit their campaign, and provide the required academic details to apply for funding.

How can I donate to a student?▶

Funders can browse campaigns, choose a student they'd like to support, and donate securely online.

Can I support more than one student at a time?▶

Yes, funders are welcome to support multiple students and campaigns at the same time.

Is my donation secure?▶

Yes, FundMyFuture uses secure payment gateways to ensure your donations are protected.

Do students need to repay the funds?▶

No, the platform is designed for micro-grants, which are donations and not loans.

How can I contact support?▶

You can reach us via the contact section in the footer or by emailing fundmyfuture@gmail.com.
